What we are

Not a gallery. Not Etsy. A place that actually works for artists.

We're a shared storefront — a small, curated group of Vermont artists selling together. You get real pricing support, no hidden fees, and a platform that doesn't make you compete with ten thousand other sellers for attention.

No listing fees. Ever.

You don't pay to be here. We only make money when you do.

Open books.

You always know exactly what we take and what you keep. No fine print.

Human-made only.

No AI art. Every piece here came from someone's hands and time.

Your work stays yours.

You keep full ownership. You can pull your work anytime, no questions asked.

Fair prices, held firm.

We set minimum price floors together. No race to the bottom.

Rooted in community.

We work with local printers and focus on artists who are embedded in Vermont life.

How it works

You make the work. We handle the rest.

Simple enough to get started quickly. Solid enough to build real income over time.

For artists

1

Apply and get selected

We read every application ourselves. We're looking for strong work and a real connection to community.

2

We set up your profile

We help you price your work, write your listing copy, and get everything looking right.

3

Your work goes live

Either in a curated drop or the ongoing storefront. We market it across all our channels.

4

You get paid monthly

One clean payment, one tax form at year end. We deal with shipping and customer questions.

For buyers

1

Browse work you can trust

Every artist here was personally selected. You're not digging through a marketplace of thousands.

2

Know who made it

Artist profiles tell you the person behind the work — where they're from, what they make, why it matters.

3

Buy knowing where your money goes

We're public about what artists earn from every sale. Most of it goes to them, and we'll always tell you exactly how much.
