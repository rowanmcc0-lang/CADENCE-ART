What we are

Not a gallery. Not Etsy.
Something that actually works for artists.

A shared storefront — a small, curated group of Vermont artists selling together. You get real pricing support, no hidden fees, and a platform that doesn't make you fight ten thousand strangers for attention.

No listing fees. Ever.

You don't pay to be here. We only make money when you do.

Open books.

You always know exactly what we take and what you keep. No fine print, no surprises.

Human-made only.

No AI art. Every piece here came from someone's hands and real time spent making something.

Your work stays yours.

Full ownership, always. You can pull your work anytime, no questions asked.

Fair prices, held firm.

We set minimum price floors together. No race to the bottom. Your work is worth what it's worth.

Rooted in community.

We work with local printers and focus on artists who are actually embedded in Vermont life.

How it works

You make the work.
We handle the rest.

Simple enough to get started in a week. Solid enough to build real income over time.
